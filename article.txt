														<h1><span >Searching Articles</span></h1>
														<span class="readingtime small">Estimated Reading Time: 3 Minutes</span>
														<div class="article-meta-new display-inline-table">
															<span class="pointer" title="Article ID"><i class="icon icon-description"></i> 80</span>
															<span title="Author: Gurjeet Kaur"  ><i class="icon icon-user-circle-o"></i>&nbsp;<span >Gurjeet Kaur</span></span>
														<span class="pointer" title="Updated On: Fri, Aug 4, 2017"><i class="icon icon-calendar-o"></i> Fri, Aug 4, 2017</span>
															<span class="info-cats"><i class="icon icon-folder4"></i>&nbsp;<span ><a href="https://www.knowledgebase-script.com/kb/category/user-manual/admin-control-panel/articles/14/" title="Categories &raquo; User Manual &raquo; Admin Control Panel &raquo; Articles ">Articles</a></span></span>

															<span >
																<div id="ARTICLECONTENT">
																	<p>To search articles in the knowledge base, click on "<strong>Search Articles</strong>" link available under <strong> Articles </strong>section&nbsp;in left navigation bar.</p>
<p><img class="img-responsive" style="border: 1px solid #999; margin-right: 40px; margin-left: 40px; border-width: 1px;" src="https://www.knowledgebase-script.com/kb/assets/search-article-link.png" alt="" width="239" height="257"></p>
<p>’Search Articles’ screen displays various filters using which you can search the article. But you need to select one or more filters to search for articles.</p>
<p><img class="img-responsive" style="border: 1px solid #999; margin-right: 40px; margin-left: 40px;" src="https://www.knowledgebase-script.com/kb/assets/search-options-part1.png" alt="" width="279" height="530"></p>
<ol><li><strong> Article ID: </strong> If you know the Article ID, you can directly&nbsp;enter it in the text box to edit it.</li>
<li><strong> Keywords: </strong> Enter the keywords that you want to search&nbsp;and then click on ’<strong>Search</strong>’ button to view the results.</li>
<li><strong> Search In: </strong> You can narrow the search more by specifying where these keywords are to be searched. By default, All is selected but you can choose any of the options among&nbsp;Title, Content, Keywords, Summary or Custom Fields.</li>
<li><strong> Categories: </strong> You can search article in specific categories using a categories drop-down. All private and public categories are embedded in this drop-down.</li>
<li><strong> Status: </strong> You can search articles of any specific status (See:<a href="https://www.knowledgebase-script.com/kb/article/types-of-articles-77.html">Types of Articles</a>) using this drop-down. By default, <strong>Any</strong> is mentioned which will search all type of articles.<br><img class="img-responsive" style="border: 1px solid #999; margin-bottom: 5px; margin-top: 5px;" src="https://www.knowledgebase-script.com/kb/assets/search-options-part2.png" alt="" width="279" height="404"></li>
<li><strong> Article Hits (Views): </strong> You can search the articles by the number of times they have&nbsp;been viewed by end users (KB Users). For this,&nbsp;specify a count&nbsp;in ’<strong>Enter&nbsp;hits</strong>’ text box and choose a comparison option from the comparison drop-down displayed above the Enter&nbsp;Hits text box. The select box has following list of options:
<ul style="list-style-type: disc;"><li>Less than - Click to search for articles viewed lesser than&nbsp;the&nbsp;number you have specified.</li>
<li>Greater than - Click to search for articles viewed more than the number you have specified.</li>
<li>Equal to - Click to search for articles that have exactly same number of views as you specified.</li>
<li>Less than or Equal to - Click to&nbsp;search for articles viewed as many&nbsp;times as you specified, or fewer.</li>
<li>Greater than or Equal to - Click to search articles viewed as many times as you specified or more.</li>
</ul></li>
<li><strong> Article Rating: </strong> You can search for articles by&nbsp;ratings given to them by end users. Select a rating number from the <strong>Rating drop-down </strong>list and comparison options (discussed&nbsp;above) from comparison drop-down list.</li>
<li><strong> Date Created: </strong> You can also search the&nbsp;article according to the date on which it was if you know created. You can specify the creation date from the date calendar.<br><img class="img-responsive" style="border: 1px solid #999; margin-bottom: 5px; margin-top: 5px;" src="https://www.knowledgebase-script.com/kb/assets/search-options-part3.png" alt="" width="284" height="465"></li>
<li><strong> Date Published: </strong> You can use this option to search for articles that are published&nbsp;before, after or on the date specified.</li>
<li><strong> Date Modified: </strong> Use this option to search for articles that were modified before, after or on the date you specify.</li>
<li><strong> Date Expired: </strong>You can specify the expiry date to search for articles&nbsp;that are expired before, after or on the date specified.</li>
<li>Click on the search button and it will display a list of results at the right side of ’Search Articles’ page as shown in the figure below.<img class="img-responsive" style="border: 1px solid #999; margin-bottom: 5px; margin-top: 5px;" src="https://www.knowledgebase-script.com/kb/assets/article-search-results.png" alt="" width="780" height="431"></li>
<li>Click on article title to preview the article and to perform the article operations click on the <strong>Actions</strong> drop-down.</li>
</ol>
